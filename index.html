About us

We are a Christian community that is built on the principles exhibited by the newly established Church in the New Testament book, Acts of the Apostles (hence our name!). We started our journey by identifying three ‘pillars’ within Acts 2:42-47 - foundational principles that the Church community was built upon. These pillars are: Spirituality (“they devoted themselves to the apostles’ teaching… and the prayers”) Service (“they would sell their possessions and goods and distribute the proceeds to all, as any had need”) Sociality (“Day by day as they spent much time together… they broke bread at home…) We are also seeking to build our Church on these three pillars. We have a commitment to healthy spirituality, both in our Sabbath worship services, and our small groups. We believe the gospel has to be lived out in practical ways which make tangible change, and so we are committed to serving the needs of others in our community, and you’re invited to come along and help us. And last but not least, we don’t overlook the value of enjoying social time together: both within small groups, and also as a larger Church community.
