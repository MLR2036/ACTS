Spirituality

We are a Church, a Christian community, a family. We are committed to living out relevant, contextual lives of faith, and sharing the good news: that God wants to have a personal relationship with every single person. Inspired by the birth of the Church, found in the New Testament book of Acts of the Apostles, our Church is built on three simple pillars: spirituality, sociality and service. Everything we do - from worship services, to volunteering in our local area, to simply getting together over food - sits atop one of these three pillars. We meet every Saturday at 11:00 for our worship service. Here you’ll experience great people, relevant messages and discussion based on the Bible, and uplifting praise through music. We're looking forward to seeing you soon!

service with us

Making a practical difference to those around us really matters to us. God calls us to be a positive agent for change! One Saturday a month, instead of having a service, we’ve chosen to be doing service. We have a variety of local projects within Waltham Forest that involve helping senior citizens, rough sleepers and the recently resettled. And we’re always working to add new initiatives. It’s our way of trying to be God’s hands and feet in the borough. We’re always looking for volunteers who want to make their patch of the world a better place! Just pop down to a Saturday worship service, and we’ll help you get involved. You can find out which weeks we are out and about volunteering through our calendar of events section, and also via our social.
