service with us

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur.

Making a practical difference to those around us really matters to us. God calls us to be a positive agent for change! One Saturday a month, instead of having a service, we’ve chosen to be doing service. We have a variety of local projects within Waltham Forest that involve helping senior citizens, rough sleepers and the recently resettled. And we’re always working to add new initiatives. It’s our way of trying to be God’s hands and feet in the borough. We’re always looking for volunteers who want to make their patch of the world a better place! Just pop down to a Saturday worship service, and we’ll help you get involved. You can find out which weeks we are out and about volunteering through our calendar of events section, and also via our social.
